# Boost figure count weight in Priority score

**Goal:** In Priority (`both`) sort mode, figure frequency should influence ranking more relative to corpus rarity.

**Context:** Score was `log(1 + figureCount) × corpusRarity`. After per-figure counting, counts are often 1–10, where `log1p` compresses differences too aggressively and lets high-rarity/low-count terms dominate.

**Change:** Use `figureCount^0.75 × corpusRarity` so frequency scales sub-linearly but with more spread than `log1p`.
